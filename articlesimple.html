NEWS BY MUGISHA MUCYO QUESNAY

Published on August 29, 2025 | Category: Technology

Kuri uyu wa Kane guhera saa kumi n'byiri z’umugoroba mu nyubako ya Grimaldi Forum iri i Monaco mu Bufaransa habereye tombola y’uko amakipe azacakirana mu mikino ibanza ya UEFA Champions League ya 2025/2026. Iyi tombola yayobowe n'abanyabigwi b'umupira w'amaguru, Zlatan Ibrahmovic na Kaka, yarangiye habonetse imikino ikomeye irimo iyo Real Madrid izakinamo na Liverpool na Manchester City. Nk'uko byagenze mu mwaka ushize w’imikino uko buri kipe yatomboye amakipe 8 ni nako izakina imikino 8 aho ine ariyo izakira mu rugo naho indi ine ikayikinira hanze. Nyuma yo gukina iyi mikino 8, muri rusange hangendewe ku manota amakipe 8 ya mbere azahita abona itike ya 1/8 naho kuva ku ikipe ya 9 kugeza ku ya 24 zihure hagati yazo maze izitsinze izindi zihite nazo zerekeza muri 1/8. Biteganyijwe ko imikino yo ku munsi wa mbere izakinwa hagati ya tariki ya 16 na 18 z’ukwezi gutaha.
